Requirements vary by country and University, but typically include: academic transcripts, English proficiency certificate (IELTS/TOEFL), a motivation letter, CV, and passport. Our platform guides you through the exact checklist for each University you apply to.

Most English-taught programmes require IELTS (typically 6.0–7.0) or TOEFL. Some Universities accept alternative proof of English proficiency. Medicine and law programmes usually require a higher score (7.0+). Our quiz takes your current level into account when matching you.
